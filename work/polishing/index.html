Polishing

INTRO

During my time at TRIUMF, I was tasked with the polishing of two large titanium electrodes. I came up with a device to help automate the process.

Background

The process of polishing titanium for high voltage environments is an involved one. These two electrodes were to be separated by less than a meter in a high vacuum, with a potential difference of 600kV between them. Needless to say, the surface finish necessarily had to be pristine.
